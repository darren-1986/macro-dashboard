Why Gold & AUD?

Australia is a top-3 global gold producer. When Gold prices rise, the Australian economy gets a massive cash injection, making the AUD stronger.

PRO TIP If Gold pumps and AUD stays flat, the AUD is "lagging" and often presents a buy opportunity.

Understanding Sentiment

Risk-On: Investors feel safe. They buy AUD and Stocks.

Risk-Off: Fear is high (Watch the VIX). Investors sell AUD and buy Gold or USD for safety.

Check the VIX Index below; if it's over 20, the market is getting nervous.

Macro Calendar Info

Watch for CPI (Inflation) and Interest Rate decisions.

If the US Fed raises rates, Gold usually drops. If the RBA (Australia) raises rates, AUD usually climbs. "High Impact" events appear in red on the calendar.
