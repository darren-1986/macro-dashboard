The Gold-AUD Link

Australia is a top gold exporter. When Gold (XAUUSD) trends up, the AUD usually follows. Look for moments where Gold moves up but AUD is still low—that is your trade signal.

Market Climate (Risk)

VXX tracks market fear. If VXX is climbing, investors are running to safety. Avoid buying "Risky" pairs like AUD/USD during high VXX spikes.

Radar Interpretation

These gauges show the "Buy/Sell" consensus. For a high-probability trade, you want the Gold Radar and AUD Radar to both point toward "Strong Buy" at the same time.
