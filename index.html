Custom Pine Script: MacroPulse Risk Oscillator

Copy the code below into your TradingView Pine Editor. This script calculates the ratio between Gold and AUD/USD to visualize global risk sentiment.

//@version=5
indicator("MacroPulse Risk Sentiment", overlay=false)
gold = request.security("OANDA:XAUUSD", timeframe.period, close)
aud = request.security("FX:AUDUSD", timeframe.period, close)
ratio = gold / aud
plot(ratio, color=color.yellow, linewidth=2, title="Fear Ratio")
hline(5000, "Pivot", color=color.gray, linestyle=hline.style_dashed)

VIX LOGIC

Current VIX (23.75) > 20. Institutional fear is high. Bias: Safe Haven demand.

GOLD PIVOT

Gold above $5,000 indicates a long-term bullish cycle. Watch for DXY weakness.

CORRELATION

AUD and Gold are inversely correlated to the DXY. When DXY rises, both usually fall.
