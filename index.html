Operational Cheat Sheet: The Macro Triangle

1. The VIX Signal

VIX > 20: Risk-Off (Buy Gold/USD, Short AUD).
VIX < 20: Risk-On (Buy AUD, Short Gold/USD).

2. Gold/AUD Ratio

Rising: Capital is hiding (Defensive).
Falling: Capital is seeking growth (Aggressive).

3. DXY Connection

The US Dollar is the pivot. If DXY is blue (Safe Haven Strong), expect resistance in Gold and AUD.

Custom Pine Script: Sentiment Oscillator

//@version=5
indicator("MacroPulse Risk Ratio", overlay=false)
gold = request.security("OANDA:XAUUSD", timeframe.period, close)
aud = request.security("FX:AUDUSD", timeframe.period, close)
plot(gold / aud, color=color.yellow, linewidth=2)
