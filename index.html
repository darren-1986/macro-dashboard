Operational Cheat Sheet: The Macro Triangle

1. The VIX Signal

VIX > 20: Risk-Off. High fear. Capital moves into USD and Gold. Short AUD.

VIX < 20: Risk-On. Confidence. Capital moves into AUD and Stocks. Short Gold.

2. Gold/AUD Ratio

A rising ratio means Gold is outperforming (Defensive). A falling ratio means AUD is outperforming (Aggressive/Growth).

3. DXY Connection

Gold and AUD both trade against the Dollar. If the USD box is blue (Strong), expect downward pressure on XAU and AUD.

Custom Pine Script: Sentiment Oscillator

//@version=5
indicator("MacroPulse Risk Ratio", overlay=false)
